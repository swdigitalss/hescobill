Manage Your HESCO Electricity Bills Online        
       
           

Easy Access to Monthly Bills

           

No more waiting for the mail—simply visit the HESCO bill website to get your latest bill instantly. All you need is your reference number, found on your previous bills. Enter this number on the site, and you can view your current bill as well as your billing history.

       
       
           

How to View, Download, and Print Your Bill

           
                   
  1. Visit the HESCO Website: Open your web browser and go to the official HESCO site.
    2.                
  2. Find the Billing Section: Look for the "Bill Information" or "View Bill Online" section.
    3.                
  3. Enter Your Reference Number: Input your unique reference number and double-check for accuracy.
    4.                
  4. View Your Bill: Click submit to see your current bill on the screen.
    5.                
  5. Download or Print: Use the options provided to download your bill as a PDF or print it.
    6.            
       
       
           

Benefits of Online Bill Management

           
                   
  • Convenience: Access your HESCO bills anytime, anywhere. Perfect for busy schedules or frequent travelers.
    •                
  • Timeliness: Receive your bill as soon as it’s generated, avoiding postal delays and ensuring timely payments.
    •                
  • Environmental Impact: Reduce paper usage by opting for electronic bills, contributing to environmental conservation.
    •                
  • Historical Records: Easily track your electricity usage and expenses with archived bills, helping you monitor and manage consumption trends.
